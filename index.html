Beagle

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Trivia

  1. Life span: 10–15 years
  2. Origin: England
  3. Temperament: Intelligent, Friendly, Merry
  4. Colors: Lemon & White, Tri-color, Chocolate Tri, White & Chocolate, Orange & White, Red & White, Chocolate & White
  5. Height: Male: 36–41 cm, Female: 33–38 cm
  6. Weight: Male: 10–11 kg, Female: 9–10 kg
